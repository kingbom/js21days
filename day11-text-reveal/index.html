Bangkok City

Bangkok city is one of the places in Thailand that never fails to amused me even after visiting this particular for more than a hundred times for the last 7 years. Whether you choose to ride the BTS or the local buses. Every corner of this city always has an amazing story to tell. I love travelling on the local buses the most if you do not mind the traffic. For a small amount of money you can take the city bus without air-conditioning, take the opportunity to snap a few shoots during the heavy traffic. A good way to experience the city culture. In the night, Bangkok is a city just like New York...never sleeps. The traffic by the night would be much better compared to the day. Take a walk on the busy streets of Silom. You will find stalls selling local merchandises and some local street food. And of course, when you come to Silom you must visit one of their bars. You will definately be fascinated and amazed by one of them. To end the night, why not sit at one the pubs and enjoy some live band music with a mug of reasonably priced beer.
